About Bella Vista

Our Story

Bella Vista, meaning “beautiful view” in Italian and Spanish, is a historic palace built in the early 20th century on a hill overlooking the serene Hussain Sagar Lake in Hyderabad. Its French-inspired architecture, sprawling lawns, and grand interiors once served as a royal retreat and later became the residence of important dignitaries.

Over the years, Bella Vista has stood as a symbol of elegance and heritage in the city. Today, it remains a reminder of Hyderabad’s glorious past, offering visitors a glimpse into the charm of European design blended with Indian history.
